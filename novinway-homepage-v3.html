What makes this different

Not an agency. Not a platform. Not clinical.

Agencies send whoever is available and move on. Platforms let you search and hope for the best. Clinical providers focus on therapy hours. Novinway is different. Our caregivers are W-2 employees - trained, supervised, and matched to your child. Whether your child needs structured after-school support or a consistent companion they actually look forward to seeing, the person who shows up knows them. If your child has been called too much or too complex for other options, that's exactly who we built this for.

Getting started

How it works

Childcare Your child's caregiver
1

Tell us about your child

A consultation about your child's needs, your situation, and what kind of care would be the right fit.

2

We build the Care Profile

Our behavior analyst gathers your child's routines, preferences, sensory needs, and what's worked before. If your child has existing support plans or strategies in place, we incorporate those into the training. This becomes the foundation.

3

We match and train

Your caregiver is selected and trained specifically for your child before the first visit. No generic orientations.

4

You step away

Go to work. Go to dinner. Sit in your car. Whatever you need. We've got it from here.

Family Support Your family's coordinator
1

Tell us what you're managing

A conversation about the logistics you're handling - providers, appointments, paperwork, waitlists, transportation - and where you need the most help.

2

We map the landscape

Your coordinator learns your child's full service ecosystem - every provider, every schedule, every deadline - so they can take things off your plate from day one.

3

Handoff begins

Your coordinator starts taking over active logistics - scheduling, follow-ups, research, paperwork - at whatever pace works for your family.

4

You stop carrying it alone

Someone else is tracking deadlines, managing waitlists, and handling the overhead. You focus on your family instead of administering it.

Starting with both? We handle it in a single intake so you're not repeating yourself. One conversation, one team, from the start.
